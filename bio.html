Who Am I?

Preacher! Author! Biker! Apologist!

I am an associate pastor at Cornerstone Community Church of the Nazarene, and a frequent guest speaker at churches and ministry events.

I am the author of 8 fiction novels, 2 non-fiction books, and 4 children's books. Find my books on Amazon!

I am an avid biker and a member of the Christian Motorcyclist Association.

I run a website dedicated to Christian Apologetics, and I often speak to groups about developing confidence in defending their faith.
